About me

The Founder & Chief Tailor- Emmystitches

xxx

Inspired by the vibrant tapestry of Nigerian culture and the dynamic pulse of its fashion scene, Emmystiches was born from a deep-rooted passion for tailoring.

As a young Nigerian, I draw strength from our rich heritage and infuse it into every garment I create. From the bustling streets of Abakaliki to Port Harcourt and to the runways of tomorrow, my journey is a testament to the creativity and resilience of our people.

At Emmystiches, we celebrate the spirit of youth, embracing individuality and crafting bespoke pieces that speak to the essence of Nigerian style.

Nnaemeka Nweda Moses
