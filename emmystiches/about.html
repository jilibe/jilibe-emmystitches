our story

Welcome to Emmystiches, where quality reigns supreme. Dive into a world of craftsmanship and elegance, where every stitch tells a story of dedication. In the heart of Nigeria, on a balmy January day in 2015, Emmystitches was born. What started as a humble dream nurtured by a young Nigerian visionary, blossomed into a thriving venture fueled by passion, creativity, and an unwavering commitment to craftsmanship.

2015 - The Inception

On January 14th, 2015, amidst the celebratory air of his birthday, our founder embarked on a journey to breathe life into his vision. Armed with a keen eye for design and a deep appreciation for Nigerian culture, he set out to revolutionize the local fashion landscape.

2016 - Laying the Foundation

In its inaugural year, Emmystitches laid the foundation for its future success. From sourcing the finest fabrics to honing the art of tailoring, each stitch woven into our garments spoke of dedication and meticulous attention to detail.

2019 - Embracing Excellence

With each passing year, Emmystitches raised the bar of excellence. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction became our hallmark, earning us recognition and accolades within the Nigerian fashion industry.

2021 - Navigating Challenges

Amidst the backdrop of unprecedented challenges, Emmystitches remained resilient. We adapted to changing times, leveraging technology to enhance our online presence and connect with our customers in new ways.

2023 - A Testament to Tenacity

As we reflect on our journey thus far, we are humbled by the support of our loyal patrons and the resilience of our team. With renewed vigor and a steadfast commitment to our craft, we look ahead to the future, eager to continue our pursuit of sartorial excellence.

Present - A Vision for Tomorrow

Today, Emmystitches stands as a beacon of creativity and innovation in the Nigerian fashion landscape. Rooted in our heritage and propelled by our dreams, we remain steadfast in our mission to redefine the boundaries of Nigerian fashion, one stitch at a time.
